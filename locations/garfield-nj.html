Immigration Medical Exams for Garfield Residents

Garfield is one of our immediate neighbors — just 3 miles and a 10-minute drive from our office. This small but vibrant Bergen County borough has a significant immigrant population, with many families originally from Poland, Turkey, Latin America, and Southern Europe all navigating the immigration process.

For Garfield residents, getting your I-693 immigration medical exam couldn't be more convenient. Dr. Michael E. Weiss, a USCIS-designated Civil Surgeon with 40+ years of experience, provides complete exams at our Clifton office for a flat $599 — with every required lab test done on-site.

Don't waste time and money at providers who charge separately for bloodwork. Our single-visit, single-price model is designed to make the immigration medical exam as stress-free as possible. Call (855) 755-1312 for a same-day or same-week appointment.

What's Included in Your $599 Exam

Your flat-fee I-693 exam at our Clifton office includes the complete physical examination, all USCIS-required bloodwork and laboratory tests, TB screening, syphilis and gonorrhea testing, vaccination record review, and your sealed I-693 form — ready for USCIS submission. No outside labs need to be visited. No hidden fees.
