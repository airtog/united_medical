Immigration Medical Exams for Union City Residents

Union City is one of the most densely populated cities in the United States and is overwhelmingly home to immigrant families — particularly from Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Colombia, and other Latin American nations. With such a concentrated immigrant population, demand for USCIS Civil Surgeon services in Union City is exceptionally high.

Dr. Michael E. Weiss's practice in Clifton is just 20 minutes from Bergenline Avenue — Union City's main commercial corridor. Our office provides complete I-693 immigration medical exams for $599 all-inclusive, and our bilingual English and Spanish-speaking staff ensures clear communication with every patient.

We know how important your immigration case is. That's why we focus on accuracy and completeness — Dr. Weiss's decades of experience mean your I-693 form is done right the first time, avoiding costly RFEs and delays. Same-week appointments are available, and your sealed form is typically ready within 5–7 business days.

What's Included in Your $599 Exam

Your flat-fee I-693 exam at our Clifton office includes the complete physical examination, all USCIS-required bloodwork and laboratory tests, TB screening, syphilis and gonorrhea testing, vaccination record review, and your sealed I-693 form — ready for USCIS submission. No outside labs need to be visited. No hidden fees.
