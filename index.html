Frequently Asked Questions

Why is your exam $599 when other places charge less?

Most practices that charge less don't include the required bloodwork and lab tests. Patients end up at Quest Diagnostics where those tests alone can cost over $1,000. Our $599 includes everything — exam, labs, blood tests, and sealed I-693 form — all done in one visit. No surprises.

How long does the exam take?

The in-office exam typically takes about 30 minutes. You'll receive your sealed I-693 form once all lab results are returned, usually within 5–7 business days.

Do I need vaccinations? Are they included?

USCIS requires certain vaccinations including MMR, Tdap, varicella, influenza, and COVID-19 depending on your age. We'll review your records and let you know exactly what you need. Required vaccinations are administered on-site during your visit.

How long is the I-693 form valid?

The I-693 form is valid for 2 years from the date the civil surgeon signs it. We recommend completing your exam as close to your USCIS filing date as possible to maximize validity.

Do you accept insurance?

The I-693 exam is a flat-fee service at $599. While most insurance plans do not cover immigration medical exams, we accept cash, credit cards, and all major payment methods.
